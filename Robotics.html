Robots are automated devices/machines that can carry out tasks they are specifically designed for. Robotics and atificial intelligence are two different technologies, however there is a connection between them that allow them to be combined to increase their potential. Artificially intelligent robots are manipulated by different artificial intelligence programs to optimise different tasks. Artficial intelligent robots can be used in healthcare, retail, education, and military. Robots intended for close interaction use a higher level of artficial intelligence. Contemporary humanoid robots have limited ability to recognize speech and mimic human emotions and facial expressions. In the future scientists believe that most robots will have artificial intelligence features included so this will help them accomplish more achievements and improve their performance. In artificial intelligent robots, the artificial intelligence is the "brain" and the sensors and parts are the "body" because of each of their different functions. Artficial intelligence robots have limited capabilities for now, but by using neural networks these capabilities can be expanded in the future.
