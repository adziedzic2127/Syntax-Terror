What is Artificial Intelligence?

Is a wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence.

There are 4 types of Artificial Intelligence:

    Reactive machines
    Limited memory
    Theory of Mind
    Self-Awareness

Artificial intelligence development has made incredible strides in the last decade.

What is Robotics?

The earliest robots as we know them were created in the early 1950s by George C. Devol, an inventor from Louisville, Kentucky. (Computer Science Stanford)

Robotics has developed so much over the last 70 years.

Robotics has astonishingly taken off since the concept was first introduced. The development of aerial drones has been utilized by the United States military to complete missions. Modern day robotics is quite beneficial to society.
